Human Development Index

The Human Development Index (HDI) is a statistical composite index of life expectancy, education and per capita income indicators, which are used to rank countries into four tiers: Very High, High, Medium and Low human development.

A country scores a higher HDI when life expectancy is higher, educational attainment is better and Gross National Income (GNI) per capita is higher. HDI helps evaluate the overall well-being and development of a country, beyond just economic growth.

This project predicts the Human Development Index of a country using machine learning based on important indicators such as life expectancy, expected years of schooling, mean years of schooling and Gross National Income per capita.
